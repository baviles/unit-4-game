Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

How the game is won is by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and it reveals it.
